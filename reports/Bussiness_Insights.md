# Business Insights

## Key Findings

- Food and Beverages generated the highest sales.
- Health and Beauty generated comparatively lower sales.
- Electronic Wallet payments were among the most preferred payment methods.
- Customer ratings remained consistently high across product categories.
- Customer segmentation identified distinct purchasing behavior groups.
- Predictive modeling demonstrated the ability to estimate future sales trends.

## Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Introduce promotional offers for low-performing product lines.
- Improve customer engagement strategies using segmentation insights.
- Continue monitoring sales trends for better forecasting.
